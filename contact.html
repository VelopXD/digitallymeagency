Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and working with us.

What is your typical process for new projects?

Our process typically includes discovery, strategy, design, development, testing, and launch phases. We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your goals and requirements, followed by a detailed proposal outlining our approach, timeline, and deliverables. Throughout the project, we maintain open communication and provide regular updates on progress.

How long does it take to complete a website project?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope, complexity, and specific requirements. A simple website might take 4-6 weeks, while more complex projects with custom functionality could take 8-12 weeks or more. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more accurate timeline based on your specific needs.

What information do you need to provide a quote?

To provide an accurate quote, we typically need information about your project goals, target audience, desired functionality, content requirements, design preferences, and timeline. The more details you can provide, the more precise our estimate will be. We're happy to guide you through this process during our initial consultation.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. Our maintenance services include regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support. We can customize a maintenance plan based on your specific needs and budget.

Can you help with content creation for my website?

Absolutely! We offer content creation services including copywriting, photography, video production, and graphic design. Our content team works closely with you to develop compelling, on-brand content that engages your audience and effectively communicates your message. We can create all content from scratch or refine existing materials to align with your brand voice and goals.
