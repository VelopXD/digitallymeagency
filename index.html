Frequently asked questions

What are your focus areas as a digital agency?

Digitallyme specializes in UI/UX design, web development, branding, and digital marketing. Our award-winning team excels in creating user-centered designs that drive engagement and conversions. We combine aesthetic excellence with strategic thinking to deliver digital experiences that not only look stunning but also achieve measurable business results. Our approach is data-driven, ensuring that every design decision supports your business objectives.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines at Digitallyme are carefully structured to balance efficiency with quality. While timelines vary based on scope and complexity, we pride ourselves on our streamlined process. Most projects begin with onboarding in less than a day, followed by a discovery phase of 2-3 days where we deeply understand your goals and requirements. Initial designs are typically ready within 4-5 days, allowing for quick feedback and iteration. Simple websites can be completed in 2-3 weeks, while complex e-commerce platforms or custom applications may take 2-3 months. Throughout the process, we maintain transparent communication with detailed progress updates and clear milestones, ensuring you're always informed about your project's status.

What is your pricing structure?

Digitallyme offers transparent, flexible pricing models including project-based, hourly, and retainer options. Our project-based pricing provides a clear, fixed cost for defined deliverables, while our hourly rates accommodate evolving projects. For ongoing partnerships, our retainer packages offer priority service and cost savings. Each quote is customized based on your specific requirements, timeline, project complexity, and business goals. We believe in delivering exceptional value and ROI for every investment. Contact us for a detailed quote tailored to your needs.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, Digitallyme provides comprehensive ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your digital products continue to perform optimally and evolve with your business. Our support services include regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, content updates, and technical troubleshooting. We offer tiered support plans to match your needs and budget, from basic maintenance to full-service management. Our dedicated support team is responsive and proactive, identifying and addressing potential issues before they impact your business. We're committed to being a long-term partner in your digital success.
